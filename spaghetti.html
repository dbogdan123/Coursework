Prawn Harissa Spaghetti



Spaghetti, is a great and well loved dish, this is why we made a healthy version with prawns instead of pork or beef. We have also added some extra ingredients to make the dish full of vitamins and even more healthy.


Broccoli: Broccolis are full of Vitamin C and K, iron and potassium. Broccolis are good for heart and eye health and they contain cancer protective compounds.

Prawns: Prowns are full of B group of vitamins and provide 22 times of Vitamin E levels of either chicken or beef. Prawns contain antioxidants that may protect against heart diesase and cancer.

Garlic: Garlic is full of Vitamin C and B6 and magnasse. Garlic can protect against illness, can reduce blood pressure and improve cholesterol levels.


The Recipe


Ingredients

  • 100g long-stem broccoli, cut into thirds
  • 180g dried spaghetti, regular or wholemeal
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large garlic clove, lightly bashed
  • 150g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 150g raw king prawns
  • 1 heaped tbsp rose harissa paste
  • 1 lemon, finely zested

Instructions

  1. Bring a pan of lightly salted water to the boil. Add the broccoli and boil for 1 min 30 secs, or until tender. Drain and set aside. Cook the pasta following pack instructions, then drain, reserving a ladleful of cooking water.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan, add the garlic clove and fry over a low heat for 2 mins. Remove with a slotted spoon and discard, leaving the flavoured oil.
  3. Add the tomatoes to the pan and fry over a medium heat for 5 mins, or until beginning to soften and turn juicy. Stir through the prawns and cook for 2 mins, or until turning pink. Add the harissa and lemon zest, stirring to coat.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti and pasta water through the prawns and harissa. Stir through the broccoli, season to taste and serve.
