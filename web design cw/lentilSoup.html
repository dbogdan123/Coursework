Spiced Carrot Lentil Soup



Nothing better than a soup that will help you prepare for your workout, that you can also enjoy after an exhausting session. This lentil soup will be exactly that. Here are the reasons why we chose these ingredients for this delicious meal.


Carrots: Carrots lower cholesterol and are full of lutein, which may reduce the risk of cancer. Carrots are also full of Iron, Vitamin C and Calcium.

Red Lentil: Red lentils are superior in providing B-vitamins with a high level of Vitamin B6. One cup of red lentils can provide 90% of the 400 micrograms of folate needed by adults.


The Recipe


Ingredients

  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 600g carrots, washed and coarsely grated (no need to peel)
  • 140g split red lentils
  • 1l hot vegetable stock (from a cube is fine)
  • 125ml milk (to make it dairy-free, use a can of reduced-fat coconut milk instead of the milk.)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large saucepan and dry-fry 2 tsp cumin seeds and a pinch of chilli flakes for 1 min, or until they start to jump around the pan and release their aromas.
  2. Scoop out about half with a spoon and set aside. Add 2 tbsp olive oil, 600g coarsely grated carrots, 140g split red lentils, 1l hot vegetable stock and 125ml milk to the pan and bring to the boil.
  3. Simmer for 15 mins until the lentils have swollen and softened.
  4. Whizz the soup with a stick blender or in a food processor until smooth (or leave it chunky if you prefer).
