Chicken Vegetable Soup



A chicken soup with mixed vegetables to give you the essential vitamins and energy you will need for a workout. The choice of ingredients of a meal is vital. This is why we chose mushrooms, chicken, zuchinni, onions and carrots for this soup. It is essentially, a low calorie vitamin and energy bomb.


Mushrooms: There are numerous benefits of consuming mushrooms often. Mushrooms contain macronutrients that support a healthy immune system. Mushrooms decrease the risk of cancer, promotes lower cholesterol and is a great source of Vitamin D.

Chicken: Chicken is a great replacement for red meat in a diet because it is rich in protein and low in fat. Chicken also contains Vitamin B12, Iron and Trytophan which helps the maintenance of the body's proteins and muscles.

Zuchinni: Zuchinni is easy to add to a diet as it can be eaten raw or cooked. It also full of Vitamin A and Potassium. Zuchinnis are not only full of vitamins and have tons of benefits, but it's also delicious, which makes it suitable for this soup

Onions: Onions help the immune system to fight off sickness and harmful bacteria, which makes this soup suitable to allow you to workout even when you're feeling sick. Onions are great source of Vitamin C, Vitamin B and potassium.

Carrots: Carrots lower cholesterol and are full of lutein, which may reduce the risk of cancer. Carrots are also full of Iron, Vitamin C and Calcium.


The Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • kosher salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil divided
  • 1 medium yellow onion diced
  • 2 celery sticks chopped
  • 2 medium carrots sliced
  • 2-3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 (15 oz.) can diced or crushed tomatoes with juices
  • 1 large sweet potato cut into small cubes
  • 1 large zucchini diced
  • 8 oz brown mushrooms sliced
  • 8 cups low-sodium chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper on both sides. Heat the oil in a large dutch oven or a soup pot and sear the chicken until golden brown on both sides. Set aside on a plate.
  2. Add the onion, celery, and carrots and saute for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the garlic, and diced tomatoes with all their juices. Add the potatoes, zucchini, and mushrooms, and pour the stock. Return the chicken back into the pot.
  3. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and allow it to simmer for 35 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the veggies are tender. Garnish with chopped parsley and enjoy!
